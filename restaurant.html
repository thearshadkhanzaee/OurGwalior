Cafés/Restaurant In Gwalior

Gwalior is renowned for its finger-licking kachoris. SS Kachoriwala is one such shop on Nayi Sadak, offering mouth-watering and freshly-made hot kachoris.

Dishes Unique in Gwalior


Poha and Jalebi are the dishes unique to Gwalior. This is the most common breakfast combination served in places around Madhya Pradesh. The piping hot Poha and Jalebi can be had during any time of the day.

Famous Snacks

Samosa, Kachori, Chat items, Dosa are some of the famous street foods in Gwalior. Desert items are also much sought after at Gwalior.
